OUR MISSION

Enactus is a network of leaders committed to using business as a catalyst for positive social and environmental impact. We educate, inspire, and support young people to use innovation and entrepreneurship to solve the world’s biggest problems.

OUR PROJECTS

Kaagaz

“Kaagaz” focuses on impacting the environment by reducing paper wastage and simultaneously creating jobs to empower the local community. The core principle behind Kaagaz is the application of the 3Rs i.e. Reduce, Reuse and Recycle.
